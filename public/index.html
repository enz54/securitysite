Who are we?

Tight Security Ltd offers you a professional service, tailor-made to your requirements. Your security is our business. All of our security officers are highly trained and are subject to constant site supervision and random inspections. We believe security is a 24/7 function that can only be achieved through robust contract management.

Our primary objectives are protection of life; protection of property and assets; detection and prevention of crime; prevention of loss and waste; and we deliver these services through our company. We at Tight Security Ltd believe in high standards of ethics, integrity and fairness in all aspects of our business and are committed to an open and honest relationship with our clients. Tight Security Ltd is fully compliant in respect of security authority legislation and industry standards and the pursuit of excellence is ongoing.
